The Process

Simple, transparent,
always in your favour

Four steps from registration to 10% saved. No fees, no catches.

1

Create a Free Account

Sign up with your name, email, and phone number — completely free. Your account gives you access to our full venue network and unlocks the discount system.

No credit card required. Registration is always free for couples.
2

Browse and Find Your Venue

Explore our curated list of Toronto's top wedding venues — from grand estates like Casa Loma to modern landmarks like the Aga Khan Museum. Filter by style, capacity, and location.

Every venue in our network is personally vetted by our team.
3

Connect With the Venue Through Us

Found a venue you love? Use our platform to send an enquiry and arrange a site visit. We facilitate the introduction — no cold calls, no unanswered emails. Tour the space and fall in love with it.

Our team is available to advise you throughout this stage.
4

Confirm Booking and Receive Your Discount Code

Once you are ready to book, return to the platform and click "Confirm Booking." Our system instantly generates a unique discount code and sends it by email to both you and the venue. Simply quote your code before signing the contract and your 10% is applied automatically.

The discount code is verified directly with the venue — no negotiation needed on your end.

Ready to start?
